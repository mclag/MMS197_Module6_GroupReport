Current Trends

in Globalization

The globalization of political economy research

The realm of Political economy research has now rapidly evolved to be more extensive and to have developed connections among scholars who are progressively concerned with issues on a global level.

Expansion of research from alternative standpoints, especially feminism and labor

Political economy has also brought further attention to areas of exploitation to provide more significant perspectives on marginalized sectors. This addresses the continuance of patriarchy and the scarcity of awareness of household and other generative labor.

Emphasis on historical research

There has been a rise in the study of non-conforming authority that opposes the norm within the government and society which leads to a greater understanding of these perspectives within these movements. Political economy broadens the definitive principle of studying the methodologies of manipulation to deal with perspectives of opposition.

The transition from an emphasis on new media, especially to the Internet

IPolitical economy has shifted from the way it has functioned in the past to a set of contemporary approaches in new media, mainly the internet. Political economists examine and consider the connection and development to better understand the enduring and new issues that need to be addressed in communication studies that are made about by old and new media.

The expansion in political activism

Lastly, there have been developments within organizations that are dedicated to researching and understanding the field of media and communication. That inturn would further contribute to the development of media and communication for the masses.
