Political Economy of Communication in Asia and Middle East

The Asian Region as a whole is generally diverse with many different societies under its own umbrella from vibrant democracies to authoritarian regimes and everything in between.

Globalization has created many upheavals with how politics and economics have played out in the regions with new major players emerging as the economic and cultural powerhouses with others becoming emerging areas of interest.
